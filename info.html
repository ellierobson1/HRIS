System Overview

The Human Resource Information System (HRIS) is a web-based Business Information System interface designed to provide employees and managers with clear access to essential HR information. This includes HR policies, benefits and leave guidance, training and development information, and contact support.

Intended Users

  • Employees
  • Line Managers
  • HR Staff

What You Can Do Here

  • Read key HR policies and procedures
  • Review benefits and leave entitlement
  • View a sample employee profile summary
  • Submit a general HR enquiry via the contact form
