System Overview

The Human Resource Information System (HRIS) is a web based Business Information System interface designed to provide employees and managers with clear access to essential HR information. This includes HR policies, benefits and leave guidance, training and development information, and ways for team members to contact support.

Intended Users

  • Employees
  • HR Staff
  • Operational Staff
  • Line Managers
  • Finance and Payroll Staff
  • IT and System Administrators

What You Can Do Here

  • Read key HR policies and procedures
  • Review benefits and leave entitlement
  • View employee profile summary
  • Submit a general HR enquiry via the contact form
